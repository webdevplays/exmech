01

Limit Order:

A singular disposable buy or sell order at a specific price.

02

Range Order:

A singular disposable buy or sell order within a customizable price range.

03

Recurring Orders:

Buy and sell orders (limit or range) linked together, akin to grid trading or a DEX trading bot. Newly acquired funds seamlessly rotate between them, establishing a perpetual trading cycle without requiring manual intervention.

04

Overlapping Liquidity:

Facilitates traders and DAOs in efficiently managing concentrated liquidity positions within specific price ranges on-chain. Leveraging Automated Market Maker (AMM) models.
